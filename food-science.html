Food Science

Understanding the science behind what we eat

The Science Behind Nutrition

Food science helps us understand how nutrients interact with our bodies, ensuring we make informed dietary choices.

What is Food Science?

Food science is the study of the physical, biological, and chemical makeup of food. It explores how food is processed, preserved, and how it affects our health.

Key Areas of Food Science

  • Nutrition: Understanding macronutrients and micronutrients.
  • Food Processing: How food is prepared and preserved for safety.
  • Food Chemistry: How different ingredients interact in cooking.
  • Food Safety: Methods to prevent contamination and foodborne illnesses.

How Food Science Improves Our Health

Scientific advancements help improve food quality, enhance nutrient absorption, and develop sustainable food alternatives for a healthier world.
